Uforse Education Technology

Uforse is an AI-powered tutoring and mentoring platform that delivers personalized learning experiences. Founded in 2016 with 3 peers, we've rapidly evolved into a million-dollar-profiting startup. In 2020, we proudly acquired our own office in Toronto. Now we have over 200 skilled instructors and mentors, and more than 1,000 active students monthly. Our vision is to help every student achieve their personal academic/career goals. We will become an unicorn and further influence more students.


Leadership Style and Management Philosophy

Being an introvert by nature, I find that I thrive in one-on-one interactions, where I can delve into deeper conversations.

I place great emphasis on effective goal setting, firmly believing that one's attitude carries more weight than mere talent. In my view, everyone deserves the opportunity to grow, and success ultimately hinges on the alignment of one's aspirations.

Recognizing my area of weakness, I acknowledge that I sometimes struggle with navigating other people's emotions. Despite witnessing improvements over time, I still identify as an introvert

Designing a clear organization structure with clear goals that aligns with everyone's career interest, with a deep understanding of every team members' strengths and weakness to fully help them grow, shapes my aporoach to leadership

Company Culture

Our company culture is guided by several core principles:

  • Begin with the End in Mind: We are dedicated to a vision-driven approach, where every action we take is aligned with our long-term goals.
  • Positive Influence: We aim to create a positive and uplifting environment that fosters growth, collaboration, and creativity among our team members.
  • Personalized Learning: We understand that everyone has unique learning needs, and we are committed to tailoring our support to each individual's growth journey.
  • Mutual Achievement:We firmly believe that helping others is, in essence, helping oneself. True achievement is only realized through collective success, where the whole team benefits.
  • Self-Evolution: Continuous improvement is at the heart of our culture. We encourage personal and professional development, empowering each team member to evolve and reach their full potential.

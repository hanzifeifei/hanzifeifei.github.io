Student Automated Assessment System (UPlanenr)

This research aims to streamline the process of selecting the right educational path for students by matching their profiles with course data using Machine Learning. The Decision Tree algorithm was chosen for this purpose due to its simplicity and effectiveness in classifying data. However, challenges arose because of the extensive database, leading to instability and inaccuracies in the algorithm's output. Existing methods such as pruning and optimization proved ineffective in handling large datasets, relying on trial and error. To address this, the research proposes an optimization algorithm to enhance the Decision Tree's speed and accuracy, making it more reliable despite the vast amount of data in the database.

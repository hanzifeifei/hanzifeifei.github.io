MYLS AI

MYLS AI builds intelligent systems that help people learn, plan, and grow in the age of AI — combining applied research with practical tools for personalized guidance and decision support.


Mission

To make humans more valuable and safe in the age of AI by developing accessible, goal-oriented AI products that support learning, planning, and professional growth.


Products and Services
  1. AI-powered personalized planning and advisory tools
  2. Applied AI research for education and workforce development
  3. Consulting and technical workshops on surviving and thriving in the AI era

Focus Areas
  • Personalized Learning: Goal-oriented, time-sensitive recommendations powered by AI and data.
  • Human-AI Collaboration: Tools that augment tutors, mentors, and professionals rather than replace them.
  • Applied Research: Bridging academic innovation with real-world products, including government-funded projects such as APPLE.
